SMARTPARK AI PARKING SERVICES

General Information

Reservation Process

Customers can reserve a parking space using the AI chatbot.

During reservation the chatbot collects:
- First Name
- Last Name
- Vehicle Registration Number
- Vehicle Type
- Reservation Date
- Reservation Start Time
- Reservation End Time

After collecting all details, the chatbot sends the reservation request to the parking administrator for approval.

Once approved, the customer receives a reservation confirmation.

Parking Availability

Parking availability changes throughout the day depending on existing reservations.

The chatbot always checks the latest parking availability before accepting a reservation request.

Parking Rules

Only registered vehicles are allowed.

Customers must park only in their assigned parking space.

Maximum speed inside the parking area is 10 km/h.

Vehicles carrying hazardous materials are not allowed.

Customers should follow all instructions provided by security personnel.

Cancellation Policy

Reservations can be cancelled up to one hour before the reservation start time.

Late cancellations may incur parking charges.

Refunds are processed within five business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I reserve a parking space in advance?

A: Yes. Customers can reserve parking spaces in advance using the chatbot.

Q: Is overnight parking available?

A: Yes. Overnight parking is supported.

Q: Are electric vehicles supported?

A: Yes. Dedicated EV parking spaces and charging stations are available.

Q: Can I modify my reservation?

A: Yes. Reservations can be modified before they are approved by the administrator.

Q: How do I know whether my reservation is confirmed?

A: The chatbot notifies the customer after the administrator approves the reservation.

Q: What information is required for reservation?

A: The chatbot collects the customer's first name, last name, vehicle registration number, vehicle type, reservation date, start time, and end time.

Data Privacy

Customer personal information is used only for reservation processing.

The chatbot never reveals confidential customer information or administrator information to unauthorized users.

Only authorized administrators can access reservation records.
